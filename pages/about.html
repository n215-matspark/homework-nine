About MPP

    Complete design and engineering services from prototype through production.

MPP provides custom-engineered powder metallurgy product solutions to customers in a variety of industries located around the world. MPP pioneered the manufacture of aluminum PM components and is at the forefront of Metal Injection Molding (MIM) technologies. Using best-in-class CNC compaction technology, we have the ability to produce components with complexity far beyond the ability of others in the industry.

We are the premiere supplier of lightweight aluminum components, which have helped MPP’s customers obtain the benefits of weight saving solutions. MPP is also a highly recognized industry leader in the supply of ferrous, stainless steel, and soft magnetic components.

    Our Mission

To create value by delighting our customers and enhancing the capabilities of the entire company.

MPP's deep applications knowledge focuses on collaborative product design, high performance materials, and the ability to convert from other metal forming processes utilizing our Conversioneering® process. Our participation in thousands of customer designs has given us an unmatched reputation in creating value by minimizing waste, energy, and lead-times, while maximizing speed to market in applications such as:

  • Hydraulic and fluid power systems
  • Gerotors
  • Gears
  • Power Transmission systems
  • Light weighting utilizing Aluminum PM
  • Intricate, miniature metal components
  • Engineered products for mechanical applications
  • And much more

      Our Vision

    Incorporating our core values, we will be the acknowledged industry leader for the supply of innovative, custom-engineered products of exceptional value.

    MPP has been producing PM components since 1948 and continues to maintain its technical leadership after seven decades of dedicated service to its customers and the industry. MPP is headquartered a few miles north of Indianapolis, Indiana where the offices for its senior management, staff, and technical center are located. Manufacturing and sales sites are in North America, Asia, and Europe. MPP is the largest industrial powder metallurgy company in North America.
